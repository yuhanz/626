California Ocean Sport Fishing Cheat Sheet 2022-2023

This cheat sheet provides a quick index to regulations. For precise regulation on on individual species, please refer to the official document - 2022-2023 Ocean Sport Fishing Regulations (PDF)

Prohibited Fin fish

  • White Shark
  • Green Sturgeon
  • Giant Sea Bass (A.K.A. Black Sea Bass)
  • Garibaldi
  • Groupers - gulf and broomtail
  • Rockfishes: bronzespotted, cowcod, yelloweye
  • Salmon - ing (a.k.a. Chinook), pink, sock- eye, silver, and chum
  • Steelhead (rainbow trout)
  • Refer to Fishing Season Closure: [Southern], [Central], [San Francisco], [Mendocino], [Northern]

No Limit Fin Fish

  • Anchovy, northern
  • Butterfish, Pacific (a.k.a. Pacific pompano)
  • Herring, Pacific
  • Herring, round
  • Jacksmelt
  • Mackerel, jack (a.k.a. Spanish mackerel)
  • Mackerel, Pacific (a.k.a chub mackerel)
  • Queenfish (a.k.a. herring)
  • Sanddabs - all except Pacific (for Pacific sanddab see “Flatfish, Other”)
  • Sardine, Pacific
  • Sculpin, Pacific staghorn (a.k.a. bullhead)
  • Topsmelt
  • Tuna, skipjack
  • Ratfish
